What is a Blog?

A blog is a website whereby information is publicated between certain time period in form of articles or POSTS ordered by dates whereby the most recent post appear at the beginning.

Characteristics:

  • The articles can contain several file types. For instance images, audios, videos and others.
  • The blog provides an interface to administrate tthe cintents and moderate the reader's comments.
  • The article has an cronological order showing the most recent first.

A blog works for several purposes, since prmotes a product or service or find a job. Each blog has a different function respectful the topic and the information related in the blog
